Welcome to the Pupil Assessment Game

In this game, you will learn how to assess pupils in an A&E setting.

You'll start by learning about:

  • Direct Pupillary Response: The constriction of the pupil in response to light.
  • Consensual Pupillary Response: The constriction of the opposite pupil when one pupil is exposed to light.
  • Relative Afferent Pupillary Defect (RAPD): A condition where one pupil reacts less than the other when exposed to light.
